About


SPExpo

SPExpo is an extraordinary event hosted by the IEEE SPS Student Branch Chapter at the College of Engineering Karunagappally, with the esteemed support of IEEE Signal Processing Society. Our mission is to introduce young minds to the captivating world of signal processing through engaging sessions and interactive workshops.

At SPExpo, students will discover the pivotal role signal processing plays in our daily lives, touching various aspects such as communications, multimedia, and medical imaging. We believe in empowering students to explore their passion for technology. That's why we've organized an exhilarating idea pitching competition, allowing participants to showcase their creative and innovative ideas related to signal processing.

The Competition



After the successful completion of the session, we offer an exciting opportunity for students to showcase their creativity and passion for technology through an idea pitching competition. Students can form a team of 3 or 4 and submit their project ideas in a comprehensive presentation format. The presentation should include the project's name, a detailed description, its purpose, estimated budget feasibility, real-life significance, and the motivation behind it. We encourage teams to present their ideas with clarity and visuals to convey their concepts effectively. Our judging panel will assess the submissions based on creativity, originality, feasibility, and potential impact. The top teams will be rewarded with exciting cash prizes. Moreover, on the same day, there will be an exhibition of college-level projects related to signal processing in our college. Schools are invited to witness these innovative projects and be inspired by the out-of-the-box ideas presented by the college students. We believe that this competition will encourage students to think critically and passionately about technology and its applications. Join us on 24th August at the College of Engineering Karunagappally and let's explore the limitless possibilities of signal processing together.
