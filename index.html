What is IEEE ?

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organisation dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.



What is SPExpo ?

SPExpo is an extraordinary event hosted by the IEEE SPS Student Branch Chapter at the College of Engineering Karunagappally, with the esteemed support of IEEE Signal Processing Society. Our mission is to introduce young minds to the captivating world of signal processing through engaging sessions and interactive workshops.

The Competition

Advanced sessions and workshops in the field of digital signal processing exclusively for college students which will be handled by expert mentors will be conducted. After the completion of the above said events we are up to organise an exhibition involving college students in which they could exhibit their projects based on signal processing inspired from the sessions we provide. The selected projects will be awarded with cash prizes. Through this idea Signal processing will become more prominent and popular among the youth. We are providing all the basic knowledge they need to get started with Signal processing and help them advance in their career.

We aim at giving basic lectures and workshops on the introduction and use of arduino to the school students, so that it could help them in doing projects on the same which pave a way to enhance their technical knowledge and bring them close towards the industrial aspects in their young age. The sessions and workshops will be mentored by expert members of our student branch chapter for smooth communication between students. Through this we aim at educating the interested students in about 6 schools in our college locality. After the mentioned events we will be conducting an idea pitching competition related signal processing inspired from our sessions taken earlier. Then selected ideas will be awarded with cash prizes.
